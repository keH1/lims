17. Модуль CRM

 
СRM – client relationship management. It's a kind of cloud-realised or local based system that allows to registrate all of actions, conversations and relationship acts between client & sales manager. LIMS U-LAB based on Bitrix24 system, that already contains CRM-module that consists three main parts:
 
1. Kanban (lead table) module where manager can see all stages the lead cards have and assign statuses that demands according to current situation with a lead. You could see the same table when start working with the deal section (because it has the same princip in basis – cards & statuses). On the canban board you can move by mouse the cards in apropriate section (column) with necessary status.
 
 
2. Lead card (information space inside the card). Here you should to fill out the fields that card contains (at least basic fields like a company and partner name, phone number, e-mail, status and basic information about your planning actions with that company and agreements you make with). You also may integrate the call recording service in your CRM. In that occasion appropriate call rec links in appropriate cards will appear. You should to make same actions in the deal section.
 
 
3. Analytics. This module allows you to analyse a lot of different parameters of sale manager's work in areas like leads, deals, clients, payments and other. You may customize the dashboard according to your analytic demands.
